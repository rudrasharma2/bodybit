# Privacy Policy for BodyBit

**Last Updated: April 28, 2026**

At **BodyBit**, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. User Content (Photos and Videos)
The primary function of BodyBit is to provide AI-powered body analysis. To do this, the app requires access to your camera to take photos or videos of your physique. 
* **Storage:** These images are stored locally on your device.
* **Processing:** Images are transmitted to third-party AI providers (OpenAI/Google) for the sole purpose of generating your analysis report.

### B. Device Identifiers
We may collect non-personal device identifiers (such as Device ID) to ensure app functionality, manage subscriptions, and prevent fraud.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data exclusively for:
* **App Functionality:** Generating your body scan reports, symmetry scores, and workout plans.
* **Subscription Management:** Processing in-app purchases and restoring access to premium features.

## 3. Third-Party Services
BodyBit utilizes industry-standard AI services to process image data:
* **OpenAI / Google Generative AI:** Used to analyze physique photos and provide fitness feedback. 
* **Data Handling:** Data sent to these providers is used only for real-time analysis and is governed by their respective privacy policies. We do not sell your data to third parties.

## 4. No Tracking & No Identity Linking
* **No Tracking:** We do not track you across other apps or websites for advertising purposes.
* **No Account Required:** BodyBit does not currently require a user account (email/password). Therefore, your data is not linked to your real-world identity in our systems.

## 5. Data Retention
* **Images:** Photos taken within the app remain on your device unless you choose to delete them. 
* **Analysis Data:** AI analysis results are stored locally on your device.

## 6. Medical Disclaimer
**BodyBit is not a medical device.** The analysis, scores, and fitness plans provided are for informational and entertainment purposes only. Always consult with a healthcare professional before starting a new exercise program or making significant changes to your health routine.

## 7. Your Rights
Since we do not store your personal data (like email or name) on our servers, we cannot identify specific users to delete data. To remove all data associated with the app, simply uninstall the application from your device.
