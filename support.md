## 2. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### How does the scan work?
BodyBit uses advanced AI to analyze photos of your physique. Simply follow the on-screen instructions to take front, side, and back photos. Our AI then processes these to estimate body fat, symmetry, and muscle proportions.

### Is my data safe?
Yes. Your photos are stored locally on your device. They are sent to our AI processing partners (OpenAI/Google) for analysis only and are not used for any other purpose. We do not require an account, so your data is not linked to your identity.

### How do I restore my premium purchase?
If you have previously purchased a subscription and need to restore it (e.g., after switching phones):
1. Open the app and go to the **Onboarding** or **Settings** screen.
2. Tap the **"Restore"** link on the paywall page.
3. Your purchase will be automatically verified with the App Store.

### I'm getting an "Analysis Error." What should I do?
Ensure you have a stable internet connection. If the error persists, try taking the photos in a well-lit area with a plain background. The AI needs clear images to provide accurate feedback.

## 3. Account & Data Deletion
Since BodyBit does not use accounts, we do not store your personal information on our servers. 
* To delete your data, simply **delete the app** from your device. 
* This will remove all locally stored photos and analysis history.

## 4. Troubleshooting
If the camera is not working, ensure you have granted camera permissions in your iPhone Settings:
`Settings > BodyBit > Camera (Toggle ON)`
